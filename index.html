Step 2 - Cooking Instructions:

Enjoy your pizza!

Ingredients: 1 pound (450g) ground beef
Salt and pepper
4 hamburger buns
4 slices of cheese
Toppings (lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, ketchup, mustard, mayo)
Instructions:
Preheat your grill or skillet to medium-high heat.
Shape the ground beef into 4 patties. Season with salt and pepper.
Cook the patties for 4-5 minutes per side for medium-rare to medium doneness. Add cheese in the last minute.
Toast the buns.
Assemble: Place patty with cheese on the bun, add your toppings.
Serve hot. Enjoy your cheeseburgers!
